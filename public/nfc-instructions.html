NFC Card Quick Guide

Report vehicle repairs in seconds

How to Submit a Repair Request

1

Tap Your NFC Card

Hold your lanyard card against the back of your phone for 2-3 seconds until a notification appears.

2

Open the Link

Tap the notification or pop-up to open the Repair Request form in your browser.

3

Fill Out the Form

Enter your name, phone number, vehicle/equipment, and describe the issue. Be specific!

4

Submit & Done!

Tap "Submit Request" and you'll receive a confirmation. The maintenance team will be notified immediately.

Quick Tips

  • Make sure NFC is enabled on your phone (usually on by default)
  • Remove thick phone cases if the card doesn't read
  • The NFC reader is usually near the camera on the back
  • Take photos of the issue before submitting if possible
