How to Submit a Repair Request

1

Tap Your NFC Card

Hold your lanyard card against the back of your phone for 2-3 seconds until a notification appears.

2

Open the Link

Tap the notification or pop-up to open the Repair Request form in your browser.

3

Fill Out the Form

Enter your name, phone, vehicle, and describe the issue. Be specific!

4

Submit & Done!

Tap "Submit Request" and the maintenance team will be notified immediately.

Quick Tips

  • NFC is usually enabled by default on your phone
  • Remove thick phone cases if card doesn't read
  • NFC reader is near the camera on back of phone
  • Take photos of the issue if possible

Need Help?

If your phone doesn't detect the card, make sure NFC is enabled in your phone settings.
